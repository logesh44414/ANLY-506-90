Title: Gapminder_income_EDA

Data description:

1. Country - Name of the country
2. Year - Year data was recorded
3. Life expectancy - Average period a person may expect to live
4. Population - Number of inhabitants recorded once every 10 years in a country
5. Income - Monetary value (in dollars) of all goods and services produced by a country in a particular time period divided by the average population for that time period
6. Region - Region the country is located in
